Journal Entry 5 - Friday December 1st
My third project was the square quilt project. For this one I had to figure out how to do a list in something that repeated. While at first I had no idea how to do that, after some research on W3Schools, I managed to make a list of colors that changed depending on the "i" value. Once again, this was increadibly fun and I can't wait for the next project.
