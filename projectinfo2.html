This is a project I made for my sister when she was staring in the Yorkville nutcracker (she has been in it for the past 8 years). It uses javascript to count the number of times you have clicked so it can alternate between a nutcracker gif and a good luck card for my sister. Additionally there is a song from the nutcracker that plays once you click. To do this I used the audio function. I hope you enjoy!
